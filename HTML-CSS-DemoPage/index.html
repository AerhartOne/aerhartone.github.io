Quick notes

If you're not a fan of Wikipedia, here's some cake facts to get you started:

  • There's a lot of types of cake.
  • Cakes are usually baked in an oven of some kind.
  • They're a good source of sugar, if nothing else.
  • Some people manage to make cakes without baking, apparently.
